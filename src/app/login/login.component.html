About Us

Electricity generation company

Intro

Kerala State Electricity Board Ltd is a public sector undertaking under the Government of Kerala, India, that generates, transmits and distributes the electricity supply in the state. Established in 1957, the agency comes under the authority of the Department of Power.

Details

Founded: 31 March 1957
Headquarters: Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, India
Owner: Government of Kerala
Owner>: State-owned enterprise
Area served: Kerala
Purpose: Electricity generation, transmission, distribution
